visual-planner

Year-view planner for Google Calendar

Open visual-planner.

To view calendar events, open the Settings and sign in to Google.

The planner only shows events from calendars which are currently selected and visible in Google Calendar. Any of the loaded calendars can then be hidden by clicking the relevant button at the bottom of the page.

Privacy Policy

When you sign in to Google from visual-planner Settings, you will be asked for permission to access your account in the following manner:

  1. View and manage its own configuration data in your Google Drive

    Allowing this access enables visual-planner to remember your app settings for each session and across your devices. It DOES NOT grant visual-planner any kind of access to your Drive documents and files.

  2. View your calendars

    Allowing this access enables visual-planner to display your calendar events in a year-view format that provides a much wider and more flexible overview of your future and past events than is available with Google Calendar.

    visual-planner DOES NOT transfer, store or share any of this data or any other personal information.
